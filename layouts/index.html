Core Principles

Your data stays yours

Vectors computed locally, never transmitted raw. Your essence is a mathematical representation that never leaves your device.

No central authority

Pure P2P networking via libp2p and Kademlia DHT. No servers to trust, breach, or shut down.

Build anything

A protocol for dating, professional networking, community matching—whatever you imagine. The infrastructure is open.
